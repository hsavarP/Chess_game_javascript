Instruction

1. The moves of a piece are done in a pair.

Eg. if u want to move a white pawn from B2 to C2, then click on cell B2 first and the click on C2

2. Important in case u click on wrong cell , suppose C3, then u need to click on B2 and then C2

Valid : click in order -> B2 -> C2

Invalid : click in order -> B2 -> C3 -> C2

Valid : click in order -> B2 -> C3 -> B2 -> C2

3. Castling has not been implemented yet.(Will be updated soon)

4. Checkmate condition has not been implemented yet.

5. The game finishes as soon as a king DIES !!!
